About Us


Engineering encompasses passion to create the wealth of our society connects us. The childish curiosity to push past our limits of knowledge challenges us. Ryerson Electronics Chapter creates a community reigniting the spark in us to have fun leveraging the theory from the lecture hall into DIY electronics projects.

REC is dedicated to promoting the engineering process of creating, developing, integrating, sharing, and applying knowledge about electrical and computer applications.

Weekly Drop-In workshops are designed to challenge participants utilizing Raspberry Pi, Arduino, Github, Electronics, Signals, Embedded Programming, design and much more of all skill levels. As an attendee, you can expect to leave each workshop with a new and improved skill set.
